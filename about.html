ğŸ¯ Project Background

This project explores the geographic distribution, thematic categories, and cultural meanings of Chinese folk tales through a digital humanities lens. By curating and digitally representing classic legends and region-specific stories, we aim to reconstruct the oral transmission paths of Chinese folklore in the digital space and promote its visual cultural heritage.

ğŸ¨ Design Concept

Guided by the aesthetic theme of "ancient charm, modern expression", the site blends ink-style motifs with responsive web design. Through map-based visualization, interactive timelines, and story categorization, we invite users to interpret the social functions, emotional narratives, and identity values embedded in each tale.
