ABOUT

Project Background

This project aims to present the geographical distribution, thematic classification and cultural connotation of Chinese folk tales in a digital humanistic way. Through the collation and digital expression of classic legends and regional characteristic stories, we hope to reconstruct the transmission path of China's oral tradition in the digital space and promote the visual inheritance of culture.

Design Concept

The overall page takes "ancient rhyme and new presentation" as the aesthetic core, integrating ink elements and modern responsive design language. Through map visualization, interactive timelines, and topic card classification, we encourage users to interpret the social value, emotional projection, and cultural identity behind the story from different perspectives.

Team involvement

We developed a visualization to show how engaged and how much time each team member is involved in the task. Charts will help to review everyone's efforts with the overall collaborative process of the project.
